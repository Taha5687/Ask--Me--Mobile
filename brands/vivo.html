Vivo V40

The vivo V40 features a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, powered by the Snapdragon 7 Gen 3 chipset. It boasts dual 50MP rear cameras with ZEISS optics, a 5,500 mAh battery supporting 80W FlashCharge, and runs on Android 14.

vivo Y28

The vivo Y28 features a 6.68-inch HD+ display with a 90Hz refresh rate, powered by a MediaTek Helio G85 processor. It boasts a 50MP main camera, a 6,000mAh battery with 44W fast charging, and runs on Android 14.

The Vivo V21 series features a 6.44-inch AMOLED display with a 90Hz refresh rate and is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 800U chipset. It has a 64MP OIS main camera, a 44MP selfie camera with OIS, and a 4000mAh battery with 33W fast charging.
