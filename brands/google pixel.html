Google Pixel 8

The Google Pixel 8 features a 6.2-inch Actua OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, powered by the Google Tensor G3 chip. It boasts an improved AI-powered camera system, 7 years of software updates, and enhanced security features like Face Unlock and VPN by Google One. 🚀

Google Pixel 10Pro

The Google Pixel 10 Pro is anticipated to feature a 6.7-inch LTPO OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, powered by the Google Tensor G3 chipset. It is expected to offer a triple rear camera setup (50MP main, 48MP ultra-wide, 48MP telephoto with 5x optical zoom) and a 10.5MP front camera. Additional features may include a 5,050mAh battery with 30W fast charging and storage options up to 1TB.

Google Pixcel 6Pro

Google Pixel 6 Pro features a 6.7" LTPO OLED display, Google Tensor chip, and a 50MP triple-camera system. It offers AI-powered photography, smooth 120Hz refresh rate, and all-day battery life..
