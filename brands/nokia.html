Nokia X20

The Nokia X20 is a mid-range smartphone offering a durable design, a 6.67" FHD+ display, and 5G connectivity. It features a 64MP quad-camera setup, a Snapdragon 480 processor, and a 3-day battery life, making it reliable for everyday use.

Nokia G10

The Nokia G10 is an affordable smartphone with a 6.5" HD+ display and a long-lasting 3-day battery. It features a triple rear camera, MediaTek Helio G25 processor, and Android 11 with two years of OS updates.

Nokia C10

The Nokia C10 is an entry-level smartphone with a 6.5" HD+ display and a sturdy design. It runs on Android 11 (Go Edition), offering smooth performance, a 5MP rear camera, and all-day battery life.
